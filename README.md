# Loanix Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 04-06-2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Loanix. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles data.

## 2. Data Collection and Use

### Local Data Storage
Loanix is designed as an offline-first tool. All loan calculations, interest rates, and history entries are stored locally on your device using an internal database (Room DB).

### Personal Information
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or bank account details.

### Permissions
The app may require standard permissions to function (such as internet access for potential future updates or crash reporting), but it does not access your contacts, location, or camera.

## 3. Data Security
Since all data stays on your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using device-level security features (like PIN or Biometric lock) to prevent unauthorized access to your calculation history.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Our app uses the following third-party libraries:

- **MPAndroidChart**: Used for visual data representation. This library does not collect user data.  
- **Google Material Components**: Used for the user interface.

## 5. Sharing of Information
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

Data only leaves the app when you explicitly choose to use the **"Share"** feature to send calculation results to another application (like Email or WhatsApp).

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
